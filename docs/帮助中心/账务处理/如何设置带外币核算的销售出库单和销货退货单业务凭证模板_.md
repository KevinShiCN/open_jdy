如何设置带外币核算的销售出库单和销货退货单业务凭证模板?

【解决方案】

点击【账务处理】-【业务凭证模板】

![](https://vip.kingdee.com/download/0109b462cb87eed949eaa7d260820d6e8921.png)
比如点击销售出库单业务凭证模板的编号,打开编辑界面![](https://vip.kingdee.com/download/0109a849f28d1c9e4aa5bdf2d1c4cfe17b50.png)

![](https://vip.kingdee.com/download/0109c7be867ce7934f8db9bed72019c7ab29.png)

![](https://vip.kingdee.com/download/0109d0b1a0b298e8482286d8b6ba82d279f3.png)
