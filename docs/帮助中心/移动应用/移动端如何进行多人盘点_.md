# 移动端如何进行多人盘点?

> 产品：金蝶云·星辰 | 分类：移动应用 | 类型：常见问题
> 作者：咚咚 | 阅读：530 | 有用：3
> 来源：[金蝶云社区](https://vip.kingdee.com/knowledge/614625763563859200)

【问题描述】

移动端如何进行多人盘点?

【解决方案】

**1、手机端登录金蝶APP。点击【应用】-【盘点】**

![](https://vip.kingdee.com/download/010994140b95b8c54062b20473dc8b0720c0.png)
**盘点单列表点<****新增****>**
![](https://vip.kingdee.com/download/010969b6e634b18f4c67b32e7598fec667fe.png)

**新增盘点单界面:盘点模式选多人盘点,盘点人选多个职员**

![](https://vip.kingdee.com/download/0109d110eaeb3b5e4eb8a0be0474a53777fe.png)
**盘点单保存以后,点击<****去盘点****>**

![](https://vip.kingdee.com/download/010973eb03d13504401c887031308e728605.png)
**分量盘点单界面,填写商品的盘点库存**

![](https://vip.kingdee.com/download/01093ad0b3c66f294109b577c6b4e6c17472.png)

**审核分量盘点单**

![](https://vip.kingdee.com/download/0109e86f61ded74b4fc58185bc4602e55497.png)

**两个职员的分量盘点单都盘完以后,回到盘点单列表**

![](https://vip.kingdee.com/download/01094484879830f344a191772dd91cda792a.png)
**点击<****汇总盘点****>**

![](https://vip.kingdee.com/download/010916712677081846dda08f039450787de6.png)
点击<选好了>

![](https://vip.kingdee.com/download/0109423ece09ff8844be9f5aa7489dd3b8c3.png)

**再点击<****审核****>盘点单**

![](https://vip.kingdee.com/download/01093236bba1ed164922a0a8f921fc0bdee2.png)
**审核完盘点单,再点击<****盈亏处理****>**

![](https://vip.kingdee.com/download/0109ee8072e2c81b47a894765201b7f6e36c.png)
**盈亏处理完就会显示盘点完成**

![](https://vip.kingdee.com/download/010903c579f5665f45048b625aa0e4f7f230.png)
