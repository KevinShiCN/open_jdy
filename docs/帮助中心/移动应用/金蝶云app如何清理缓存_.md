【问题描述】

金蝶云APP如何清理缓存?

【操作指引】

1、登录金蝶云app后点击“我的”,点击右上角小齿轮![](https://vip.kingdee.com/download/010921528aade83047eb9d8061d6f16dc243.png)设置;在设置界面点击下方“清理缓存”按钮

![](https://vip.kingdee.com/download/010968d51327fe8a456fb65b5c787de28787.png)![](https://vip.kingdee.com/download/010947ae5ef7ca084329aa5547fa8e607f97.png)

2、系统弹窗提示:清理本地内容缓存,点击【清理】,清理完成时,系统将提示:清理成功

![](https://vip.kingdee.com/download/0109fef4437ca2f4490298a6f54749936403.png)![](https://vip.kingdee.com/download/0109a1bb5e5797284feb8fdc7c6c966d7eb8.png)
