![](https://vip.kingdee.com/download/01098e79708aa05f4078ba8266d84a9f3f1e.png)

【处理流程】点击系统设置-用户管理,选择需要授权的用户,勾选用户,点击授权

![](https://vip.kingdee.com/download/01092a46d42f27cf4d73afabebeb9926ce72.png)

![](https://vip.kingdee.com/download/0109faf65854c892430595049250f320df35.png)
